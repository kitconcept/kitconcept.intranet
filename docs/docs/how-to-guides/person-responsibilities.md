# Person Responsibilities

The kitconcept intranet distribution provides a **responsibilities** field on the Person content type.
This field allows users to describe what topics, tasks, or questions they are responsible for, helping colleagues find the right contact person.

## Field Location

The responsibilities field is located in the "Responsibilities & Expertise" fieldset on the Person edit form.

## Usage

Enter free-form tags describing your responsibilities. Examples:
- "Budget planning"
- "IT-Support"
- "Onboarding"

The field supports autocomplete suggestions based on existing responsibilities in the system, making it easier to use consistent terminology across the organization.

## Searchable Index

Responsibilities are indexed in the catalog, allowing users to search for persons by their responsibilities.
